SUMMARY

Rental apps are an inescapable part of adulthood. As a user of them myself, I tend to find them a bit cluttered. By necessity, rental apps tend to be information rich. There’s a lot people want to know about their new potential home, after all! My idea with this new rental app was to change the way listing information is given to the user by making it customizable. For example, a single twenty-something like myself doesn’t care about nearby schools. This app lets the user decide what information is shown.
