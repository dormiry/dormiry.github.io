Rx Saver App

YEAR

2020

TIME

4 Weeks

TOOLS

Figma

SUMMARY

Prescription (Rx) coupon apps tend to lack the level of polish needed to seem real, legit, and user-friendly (in other words, they look kind of sketchy!) The goal with this project’s design is to possess a level of polish that makes the interface extremely straight-forward & easy to use. It’s unlikely the user will spend a lot of time in the app, so the fewer barriers they experience, the better.
