10 Weeks

TOOLS

SUMMARY

The American Dream Network is the culmination of two terms worth of thesis work. The first term was spent researching the American Dream—when it originated, what it means, and who tends to believe in it. I turned that research into an expression of people’s frustration with it. Using collage, motion graphics, and coding, I was able to create an interactive gallery of the “Lifespan of the American Dream.” It was a labor of love and an exercise in irony.
